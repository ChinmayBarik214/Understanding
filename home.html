CBT online
World renowned online therapists, counselors, and coaches specializing in cognitive behavioral therapy.

The world’s largest platform dedicated to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for clients and clinicians.

More effective than traditional talk therapy.

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is an evidence-based approach to mental and emotional wellness that actually works. Our therapists and coaches use scientifically proven methods to teach you the skills you need in order to break negative patterns and habits. Through directive sessions you’ll build progress as you learn and practice actionable skills for improving all areas of your life.
